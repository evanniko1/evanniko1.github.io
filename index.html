About Me

I am currently the Head of AI at Myria Biosciences, where I lead cutting-edge initiatives at the intersection of artificial intelligence and biotechnology. With a PhD in Quantitative Biology from the University of Edinburgh, I specialize in next-generation computational optimization of protein expression systems and deep learning applications in biological contexts.

My research has been published in Nature Communications and other leading journals, focusing on accuracy and data efficiency in deep learning models of protein expression. I develop machine learning approaches for biological sequence analysis, protein engineering, and synthetic biology applications.

With expertise spanning from Harvard University to Imperial College London, I bring a unique blend of computational prowess and biological insight to solve complex challenges in modern biotechnology. I'm passionate about developing AI tools that accelerate biological discovery and therapeutic development.
